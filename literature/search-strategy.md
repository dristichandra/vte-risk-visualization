## Search Terms
- ("venous thromboembolism" OR "VTE" OR "blood clot") AND ("visualization" OR "risk communication" OR "clinical decision support")
- ("dynamic risk prediction" OR "temporal risk") AND ("cancer" OR "oncology")
- ("uncertainty visualization" OR "risk display") AND ("clinical" OR "medical")

## Inclusion Criteria
- Published in peer-reviewed journals or conference proceedings
- Focus on dynamic risk prediction, uncertainty visualization, or clinical decision support implementation
- Available in English

## Exclusion Criteria
- Studies focused solely on static risk scoring without visualization
- Non-clinical applications of uncertainty visualization
- Conference abstracts without full text
